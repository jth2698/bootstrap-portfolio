About Me

As a business lawyer, James is often too serious about himself and others. He often refers to himself in the third person, particularly when writing. The important things about him are this: he is from Louisiana and is therefore not to be trusted; he is a lawyer which should deepen suspicion; he is learning web and app development, so there is at least one redeeming factor.

James is fascinated by politics, business, and the operation of our crazy, modern world. He hopes to develop web apps that might one day change the world, or at least make a modest return.
